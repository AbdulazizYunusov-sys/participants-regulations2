Participants regulations

1) Delegates must attend all days and full sessions of the conference.

2) Any absence must be communicated in advance and approved by the organizing team.

1) Formal Western business or Casual attires are required at all times during the conference.

1) All participants must show respect to chairs, staff, and participants at all times.

2) Disrespectful behavior, personal attacks, or offensive language are not tolerated.

3) A cooperative, diplomatic tone is expected in all discussions and interactions.

1) All work submitted (position papers, draft laws, resolutions) must be original.

2) Plagiarism or copying content are strictly prohibited.

3) Violations may lead to disqualification and removal from the event.

1) Only a formal language should be used throughout the event and in committee groups.

2) Delegates must not attempt to influence chairs, staff, or awards decisions informally.

3) Any form of lobbying or pressure related to results is prohibited.

First warning: formal reminder and record of the violation.

Second warning: impact on evaluation and eligibility for awards.

Third warning: removal from the committee.

1) Delegates must respect the venue, property, and event staff at all times.

2) Littering, vandalism, or inappropriate behavior in venue areas (halls, restrooms, cafeteria) is prohibited.

1) Regardless of age, the use or possession of alcohol, cigarettes, electronic cigarettes (vapes), or any illegal substances is strictly prohibited within the premises of the conference venue.

2) Delegates must maintain respectful and appropriate behavior at all times while on-site, including during breaks and outside of committee sessions.

3) Any behavior that compromises the reputation of the event or safety of participants, including intoxication or inappropriate conduct, will result in immediate removal from the conference without warning.
